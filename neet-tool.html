Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to resize 4x6 postcard photo for NEET 2026?

To resize a 4x6 postcard photo for NEET 2026:

  1. Select "Postcard Size (4x6 inch)" mode in this tool
  2. Upload your photograph (taken after January 1, 2026)
  3. The tool automatically adjusts dimensions to 4" x 6" (10.16 x 15.24 cm)
  4. Compression algorithm ensures file size stays between 10KB-200KB
  5. Download the NTA-compliant JPG file

Important: The postcard photo must be identical to the passport size photo uploaded in the application form. You must carry 4-6 copies to the exam center for pasting on the attendance sheet and admit card.

What is the difference between passport size and postcard size photo in NEET?

The passport size photo (2.5"x3.5") is uploaded during the online application process. The postcard size photo (4"x6") is larger and must be pasted on the NEET admit card printout and carried to the exam center. Both must be the same image, recent (after Jan 1, 2026), with white background and 80% face coverage.

Why is my NEET photo getting rejected?

Common rejection reasons include:

  • File size outside 10KB-200KB range
  • Photo taken before January 1, 2026
  • Background not plain white or light-colored
  • Face coverage less than 80% or ears not visible
  • Wearing cap, goggles, or sunglasses (unless medically required)
  • Computer-generated or morphed images
  • Blurry or pixelated quality
