How to Resize Photo for SSC Online Application?

Uploading the correct photo size is crucial for SSC applications. A common mistake is uploading a high-resolution image directly from a phone. Use our SSC Photo Resizer tool to automatically adjust your image to the required dimensions (typically 20 KB to 50 KB) and resolution (100x120 pixels).

UPSC Signature Resize Guidelines

For UPSC exams like IAS, IPS, and IFS, the signature file must be between 20 KB and 300 KB. The dimensions should be approximately 140 x 60 pixels. Our tool ensures the aspect ratio is maintained while compressing the file size to meet these strict requirements.
